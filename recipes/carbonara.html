Carbonara

Description

  • Bacon 300g
  • Eggs 2
  • Pasta you like 500g
  • Onions 2 medium size
  • Cream
  • Cheese 100g (Advised Parmesan)
  1. Boil a pasta
  2. Dice bacon and onions
  3. Fry bacon on the pan Bacon don't need oil or olive just wait for fat to melt
  4. When fat melt from bacon add diced onions
  5. Fry it for 2 min then add cream and cheese
  6. Add some seasoning you like and stir the sauce garlic is great additiion
  7. When sauce is boilnig switch burner off
  8. Separate yolk from whites and add yolk to sauce
  9. Stir it and switch a burner on to the point when sauce start to boil
  10. Add pasta to sauce stir until combine
  11. Enjoy you meal!
