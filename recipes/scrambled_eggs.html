Carbonara

Description

  • Eggs large 2
  • Cream 100 ml (Advised 30%)
  • butter 50g (You could also use oil)
  1. Stir eggs and cream in small bowl
  2. Heat butter (or oil) on frying pan
  3. Pour mixture from bowl to heated frying pan
  4. Stir every 20 second until eggs are set
  5. Enjoy you meal!
