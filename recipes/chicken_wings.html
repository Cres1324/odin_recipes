Chicken wings

Description

  • Chicken Wings
  • Baking Powder
  • Salt
  • Garlic powder
  • Unsalted butter
  • sugar
  • Hot sauce
  1. Cut chicken wings in half and preheat them
  2. Combine baking powder,salt, and garlic powderinto dry seasoningng
  3. Season the wings with dry seasoning
  4. Bake the wings for 50 mintutes flipping halfway
  5. Mix melted butter sugar and hot sauce
  6. Toss the wings into the sauce
  7. Enjoy you meal!
